TestParticle.prepare — Method
prepare(E, B; species=Proton, q=1.0, m=1.0)

Return a tuple consists of particle charge, mass for a prescribed species and analytic EM field functions. Prescribed species are Electron and Proton; other species can be manually specified with species=Ion/User, q and m.

TestParticle.prepare — Method
prepare(E, B; species="proton", q=1.0, m=1.0)

Return a tuple consists of particle charge, mass for a prescribed species and analytic EM field functions. Prescribed species are "electron" and "proton"; other species can be manually specified with species="other", q and m.
